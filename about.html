Who we are

Leading international trading company

Mandril S.R.L. is a professional trading company specializing in import and export operations in industrial and agricultural sectors. We connect businesses across international markets with quality products and comprehensive support services.

Contact us

01.Our Mission

To provide professional trading solutions that connect businesses across borders, delivering quality products and exceptional service in the industrial and agricultural sectors.

02.Vision

To become the leading trading partner for businesses seeking reliable import-export solutions, recognized for our integrity, expertise, and customer-focused approach.

03.Our Approach

We combine market knowledge, strong partnerships, and professional logistics to deliver customized solutions that meet each client's specific needs and requirements.
